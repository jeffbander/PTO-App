CSV Format Requirements
Required Columns:
Column Name Format Example
name Full name John Doe
email Email address john.doe@mountsinai.org
position Position name CVI RNs, APP, Front Desk/Admin, etc.
team Team (admin or clinical) clinical
pto_balance_hours PTO balance in hours (decimal) 60.0
pto_refresh_date Date format: YYYY-MM-DD 2025-01-01
Important Notes:
  • If an email already exists, the employee's data will be updated
  • New positions will be created automatically if they don't exist
  • Team must be either "admin" or "clinical" (case-insensitive)
  • PTO balance is in hours (7.5 hours = 1 day)
